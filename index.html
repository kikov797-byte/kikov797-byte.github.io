About us

We are a non-profit founded in 2026. Our goal is to educate young minds about systems of the body through fun, engaging activities. We host 4-week camps directly at the student's school, primarily serving 4th–6th graders.

Mission

Our mission is to not only educate youth about body systems, but to also guide them in exploring future career paths — emphasizing curiosity, learning, and growth.
